About Us

Your Neighbors, Your Fire Company

Port Matilda Fire Company #15 is an all-volunteer organization that has been serving the Port Matilda area since 1929. Our members respond to structure fires, vehicle accidents, medical emergencies, and more — all while balancing their own careers and families.

Our primary coverage area is approximately 81 square miles with a population of over 5,600 residents, covering Port Matilda Borough and Halfmoon, Huston, Taylor, and Worth Townships. We also respond to surrounding areas to assist other fire and EMS companies.

We rely on community support to operate. Municipal contributions cover only part of our budget — fundraising and donations from people like you make up the rest.

Stay Safe

Safety Tips

Simple steps to protect your home and family.

🔥

Smoke Detectors Save Lives

Test smoke detectors monthly and change batteries at least once a year. Install detectors on every level of your home and inside bedrooms.

🧯

Fire Extinguisher Basics

Keep a fire extinguisher on each floor and learn the P.A.S.S. technique: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep.

❄️

Winter Hydrant Access

Help us save time in an emergency — clear snow at least 3 feet around fire hydrants near your property.

🚪

Plan Your Escape

Create and practice a home fire escape plan with two exits from every room. Designate a meeting spot outside.
